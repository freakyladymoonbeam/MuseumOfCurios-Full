# Museum of Curios

**Museum of Curios** is a narrative-driven collectathon and museum simulator game where players curate a unique museum filled with extraordinary and mysterious artifacts—referred to as **curios**—from across the world.

---

## Game Concept
In Museum of Curios, players will:
- Discover and collect curios of varying rarity, from **Common** trinkets to **Mythical** relics.
- Travel around a Dragon Quest-inspired fantasy world to learn the history of each area and locate curios to display.
- Expand and customize the museum, showcasing the odd, magical, and historically significant curios they find.
- Complete quests and character storylines to unlock special curios.
- Interact with a wide variety of characters, many of whom have their own quests and narrative arcs.

Curios are categorized by rarity:
- **Common** – Souvenirs or easily obtainable items.
- **Rare** – Unique or scarce items; sometimes quest rewards.
- **Epic** – Regionally famous artifacts, sometimes tied to character quests.
- **Legendary** – World-renowned, often magical items tied to major quests.
- **Mythical** – Extremely storied and often considered lost; obtained via special mid-to-late-game quests.

---

## Current Features
- Basic console-based gameplay loop
- Curio collection system with rarity tiers
- Interactive catalog for browsing collected curios
- Placeholder structure for quests and character interactions

---

## Future Plans
The project is in active development, with plans to:
- Implement a richer quest and story system for characters
- Implement the romance system with dynamic NPC-to-NPC interactions
- Add a graphical interface for browsing the museum and curios
- Introduce crafting, combining, or modifying curios
